# Divine Pharmacy - Modern Pharmacy Management System

Divine Pharmacy is a state-of-the-art "Offline-First" Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) system. It is specifically designed for pharmacies that require 100% uptime, even when the internet is unreliable.

## Brand Identity & Clinical Design
The system uses a "Clinical Design System" that feels modern, premium, and trustworthy. 
- **Core Teal (#54ACBF)**: Represents health, clarity, and precision.
- **Deep Space Blue (#011C40)**: Used in the sidebar to provide a stable, professional anchor to the interface.
- **Success Green (#33B26B)**: Clearly indicates positive outcomes, such as completed sales or replenished stock.
- **Lightest Blue (#A7EBF2)**: Used for soft highlights and subtle backgrounds to keep the interface clean and "breathable."

## Why Divine Pharmacy is Different (For Non-Technical People)

### 1. The "Always-On" Sales Counter
Unlike many systems that "freeze" or stop working if the internet goes out, Divine Pharmacy stays alive. You can continue ringing up customers and making sales without an internet connection. Once your connection returns, the system automatically pushes all your sales to the cloud in the background.

### 2. High-Precision Inventory Control
The system doesn't just track "how many bottles" you have. It tracks **batches** and **expiry dates**.
- It alerts the team when items are reaching their reorder level.
- It prevents the sale of expired medications by flagging them automatically.
- It synchronizes stock levels across all devices instantly when internet is available.

### 3. Professional Reporting (The "Business Pulse")
Owners and managers get a real-time view of daily revenue and top-performing products. 
- **Daily Summaries**: See exactly how much cash vs. digital payments were made.
- **Popular Products**: Understand what’s selling most so you never miss a restock opportunity.

### 4. Staff Security & Modules
Every staff member has a specific role. 
- **Staff Mode**: Focuses strictly on selling and basic product search.
- **Manager Mode**: Unlocks the "Back Office" – full financial reports, supplier management, and staff settings.

### 5. Supplier / Procurement Management
Keep clean records of every order placed with suppliers. Track what has been paid for, what is pending, and what has arrived, ensuring your supply chain is never a mystery.

## The Digital Transformation (Replacing Manual Logs)
Divine Pharmacy is built to replace manual, paper-based logging. By digitizing your workflow:
- **Zero Calculation Errors**: No more manual math at the end of the day.
- **Instant Search**: Find any product in seconds rather than flipping through pages.
- **Data Safety**: Your records are backed up to the cloud automatically, protecting you from fire, theft, or physical damage to paper records.
